What is Donutroplis?

Welcome to Donutropolis, an engaging online world where you can:

• Work and Trade: Earn and trade Sprinkles with other players.

• Build Your Home: Design, customize, and upgrade your home.

• Play Mini-Games: Enjoy a variety of exclusive mini-games.

• Join the Community: Connect with other players on Discord.

All of this is available for free and from the comfort of your browser! Expect regular updates that add much more content as the community grows. Jump into Donutropolis today!
